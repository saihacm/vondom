HOTEL MONGROVE SANYA

The new 7-star hotel, Mangrove, located in the island of Sanya, Rep of China, is a paradise in the middle of the Southern China Sea, where art, luxury and elegance are everywhere.

Each of the Hotel hallways has artworks by West Pike Milan, having as well the world’s longest artwork by this artist.

At the hotel there is an underwater church, suitable for exclusive and original weddings.

The Diamond Bar has a glass roof through which you can see the clouds, the sun… but if looking at the hotel from the air, it looks like a diamond embedded in the sky.

Each of the 29 villas has been designed with exquisite style to make each guest feel exclusive. Outside each of them, Vondom’s outdoor furniture: Doux sofas, Faz sun chaise, Bum Bum, Vela daybeds, Rosinante… is what provides relaxation along with the sea view, either enjpying a cocktail or just relaxing.

In the hotel’s various restaurants, design tables and chairs of the Wing, Ufo and Pezzetina collections have been chosen for the dinning comfort, while being delighted with delicious and exquisite dishes prepared by international chefs.

Around the pool you will find the Rest Sun chaises. And in the bar, alongside the pool are the stools and tables of both Wing and Spritz collections.

In the surroundings of the hotel there are bungalows, where guests can enjoy, have a refreshing drink and chat around Moma tables.

Additionally, the hotel has a business center, shops, wrestling club, bars, spa… where guests can enjoy an endless number of activities without leaving the hotel.

Definitively, a luxury designed resort really close to paradise.
